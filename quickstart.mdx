## Overview

Get started with Duyên Phan Documentation in minutes. This guide walks you through account creation, logging in, setting up a new documentation space, uploading your initial content, and basic navigation. You will have a fully functional docs site ready for your project.

<Callout kind="info">
Duyên Phan supports MDX for interactive documentation with components like Steps, Tabs, and Cards.
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- Your MDX files or markdown content prepared
- An email address for account verification

## Step-by-Step Setup

Follow these steps to launch your first documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">

    Navigate to `https://app.duyenphan.com/signup`.

    1. Enter your email address.
    2. Choose a strong password.
    3. Complete the CAPTCHA if prompted.
    4. Check your inbox and click the verification link.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">

    Go to `https://app.duyenphan.com/login`.

    Enter your verified email and password.

    You land on the dashboard.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="plus">

    From the dashboard, click the `New Space` button.

    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`.

    Select `Public` or `Private` visibility.

    Click `Create` to generate your space.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Initial Content" icon="upload">

    In your new space, use the `Upload Files` drag-and-drop area.

    Add these starter files:

    <CodeGroup tabs="index.mdx,quickstart.mdx">

    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Welcome to My Docs
    description: Your project documentation home.
    ---
    
    ## Introduction
    
    Start documenting your project here.
    ````

    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Quickstart
    ---
    
    Follow the steps to get running.
    ````

    </CodeGroup>

    Duyên Phan auto-processes MDX files.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate and Edit" icon="edit-3">

    Use the left sidebar for page navigation.

    Click the pencil icon on any page to enter edit mode.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Edit Mode" icon="edit">

        Make changes directly in the MDX editor.

        Preview renders live with components.

      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Preview Mode" icon="eye">

        Toggle to full preview.

        Test interactive elements like Tabs and Steps.

      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    Save with `Ctrl+S` (`Cmd+S` on macOS).

  </Step>
</Steps>
